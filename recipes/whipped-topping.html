Whipped Topping

Description

This recipe is as delicious as it is simple. It's a 3-ingredient no fuss, no muss delight! A wonderful pairing for ice creams, cakes, puddings, and so much more, you'll wonder where it's been your whole life!

Whipped topping is so versatile and takes on any flavoring you add to it, so it's completely customizable! Just swap in your favorite extract(s). You do you!
