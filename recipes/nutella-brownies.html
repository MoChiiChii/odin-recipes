Nutella Brownies

Description

If there is one singular truth in this world, it is that the smell of fresh-baked brownies wafting through the air is one of life's simple pleasures. These Nutella brownies are as indulgent and delicious as they are incredibly simple to make.

Now I love chocolate, and these brownies do not disappoint. They are a family treat and pair wonderfully with a tall glass of milk. They are a guilty pleasure of mine, and I hope you enjoy them as much as I do!
